Flare Gun:

The Flare Gun in PUBG is a rare find in all the three maps that have been rolled out in the game yet (Erangel, Miramar and Sanhok) and is used to call-in a special supply-crate drop (when you are inside the white circle) or an armoured UAZ (when you are outside the white circle).
